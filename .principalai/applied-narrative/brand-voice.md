## Testing Your Copy

Use this checklist when writing for Principal AI:

- [ ] Does it sound like us? (Check voice attributes)
- [ ] Is the tone appropriate for the context?
- [ ] Does it align with our messaging pillars?
- [ ] Have I avoided jargon or explained technical terms?
- [ ] Would our target audience understand and connect with this?
- [ ] Is it clear what action we want the reader to take?
- [ ] Have I followed our style guidelines?
- [ ] Does it respect and include our audience?
